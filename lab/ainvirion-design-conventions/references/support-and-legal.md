# Support & Legal

Immutable data shared across all AInvirion products. No landing page may modify these values.

## Contact information

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Support URL** | `https://github.com/AInvirion/support/issues/new/choose` |
| **Phone** | (425) 276-7365 |
| **Company** | AInvirion |
| **Locations** | Seattle, USA · Santiago, Chile |

## Copyright

Required format in every footer:

```
© {year} AInvirion | Seattle, USA · Santiago, Chile
```

- `{year}` = current year
- Always reference **AInvirion**, even if the product has its own branding
- Include both locations (Seattle + Santiago)

## Required legal links

Must appear in `.footer-legal-links` on every landing:

1. **Privacy Policy** — `href="#"` (placeholder until page is created)
2. **Terms of Service** — `href="#"` (placeholder until page is created)

## Contact CTA rule

**Rule**: every button or link labeled "Contact", "Get support", "Book a call", or any support CTA must redirect to:

```
https://github.com/AInvirion/support/issues/new/choose
```

Open in new tab with `target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"`.

The phone number is optional in the footer (`.footer-contact`), but if shown, it must be (425) 276-7365.

## Congruence checklist

When creating or reviewing a landing, verify:

- [ ] Copyright says "AInvirion" with both locations (Seattle, USA · Santiago, Chile)
- [ ] Support link points to GitHub Issues (not email or custom form)
- [ ] Privacy Policy and Terms of Service are present in footer
- [ ] Phone number, if shown, is (425) 276-7365
